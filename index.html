Our Mission

Cultivating an innovative, diverse and supportive community through active learning.
Innovation

Makerspaces are synonymous with creativity, experimentation and innovation. In a makerspace, bringing together passionate, creative people literally builds great things.

Diversity

Makerspaces can be an important tool for encouraging diversity in the STEM fields by creating a safe, welcoming space. Informatics is committed to following proven practices for promoting diversity.

Entrepreneurship

Connect with your fellow students, craft a business plan and pitch your idea to vetern entrepreneurs. Our rapid prototyping tools allows students to take an idea and make it a reality!

Community

Our central focus is cultivating a supportive community through active learning. Whether it's building a flying drone, or 3D printing a replacement part, we strongly believe we are more effective solving problems together.

About US

Building a Foundation for Success. Making a Difference.
Developing Amazing Things Together with Passion.

Innovation in Informatics is a makerspace, that is a physical location where people gather to share resources and knowledge, work on projects, network and build. Makers leverage just about any tool to build their idea, including: software, gadgets, circuit boards, sensors, duct tape, wearable computers and 3D printers. Primarily these are places for technological experimentation, hardware development, and idea prototyping.

Informatics is excited by the launch of this new space, enabling the department to build on existing innovative practices and community engagement.

Check out our event section and join us. Our space is built on the dedication and commitment of our students, faculty, industry partners and dedicated alumni.
